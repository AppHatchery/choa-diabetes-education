Managing low blood sugar

Symptoms for hypoglycemia

Symptoms

Shakiness

Sweating

Dizziness

Weakness

  • Low blood sugar can happen at anytime, be sure to always be prepared and carry your diabetes supplies with you, including your meter, supplies to treat a low blood sugar, and insulin.
  • Caregivers should always roll child on their side after glucagon administration because this medicine can induce vomiting.
