How to count carbohydrates

Now that we know which foods contain carbs, and the effect that fat and protein also have on our blood sugar, let’s learn how to count carbs.


Ways to count carbs

  • 1. Count in grams
  • 2. Reading food labels
  • 3. Look at serving size—all information on label is for serving size listed
  • 4. Look at total carb grams

NOTE
If eating more than one serving make sure to do the math to understand how many carbs are in the amount you’ll eat!

Example
If you have 2 servings of the food, the cards should be 27x2=54g
