How to calculate insulin dosages


Long-acting insulin

Long-acting insulin (Lantus/Levemir/Basaglar/Tresiba/Semglee)- It is a set dose prescribed by your care team. The same dose of this insulin is given at the same time, every day regardless of what blood sugar is.

Rapid-acting insulin

Rapid acting insulin (Humalog/Novolog/Ademelog/Fiasp)- needs to be calculated whenever your child eats carbohydrates and when blood sugar is elevated at meal time.

We need rapid acting insulin for two reasons, carbohydrates and to correct a high blood sugar.
