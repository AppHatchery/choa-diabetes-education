How to check blood sugar

Gather Supplies

Glucose meter

Lancets

Lancing device

Test strips


Steps to Check Blood Sugar

Wash your hands with soap and water, then dry them well.

Place a new lancet in the lancing device.

Put a test strip into the meter.

Hold the lancing device to the side of your finger and press the button to get a drop of blood.

Touch the blood drop to the edge of the test strip, following your meterâ€™s directions.

Read and record your blood sugar result.

Remove and safely throw away the used lancet in a sharps container.
