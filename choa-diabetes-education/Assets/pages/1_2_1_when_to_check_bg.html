When to check blood sugar?

Checking blood sugar helps you understand what your body needs next. It shows if you need insulin, a snack to treat a low, or if it’s safe to play or exercise. It’s an important way to keep blood sugar in a healthy range.

You may need to check your blood sugar:

Before meals

Bedtime

At 2 am (for a little while after diagnosis; your diabetes doctor will tell you when to stop checking at this time)

Before exercising

When experiencing symptoms of low or high blood sugar

When sick
