Storage of insulin

It is very important for insulin to be stored properly. If not, it may not work as it is supposed to.

Unopened insulin should be stored in the fridge and is good until the expiration date on the box as long as it is unopened.

Always check the expiration date on the vial or pen before you use it. Throw it away if it is expired.

Once a vial or a pen of insulin is opened, it is good for 28 days at room temperature.

It can be helpful to label an insulin pen or vial with the date you opened it with a sharpie
