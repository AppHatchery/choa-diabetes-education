Main types of Diabetes.

Type 1 diabetes happens when an autoimmune response kills the cells in our body responsible for making insulin, therefore no insulin is made and glucose cannot get out of the blood and into the cells to be used for energy.

Type 2 diabetes happens when your body cannot use the insulin it makes or when your body does not make enough insulin. You may have to take insulin or another type of medicine to keep your blood sugar in a healthy range.
