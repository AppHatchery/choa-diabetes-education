Low Carb Snack Combinations

5 grams or less of carbohydrate


Two Ingredient Snack

  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes + 1 string cheese.
  • ½ cup cucumber slices + 1 Tbsp hummus
  • 1 graham cracker square + 1 tsp nut butter
  • 3 pieces of salami + 1 cheese stick
  • 1 hard-boiled egg + ¼ cup blueberries
  • Tuna pickle boats: 2 dill pickles with centers scooped out + 1-2 Tbsp tuna salad
  • Lettuce wraps: ½ cup chicken or tuna salad + 2 lettuce leaves
  • Bell pepper boats: ½ cup bell pepper slices + 2 Tbsp goat cheese (spread on top)

Three Ingredient Snack

  • ½ cup cottage cheese + ¼ cup sliced cucumber + 1 Tbsp chopped walnuts.
  • 1 cup popcorn + 1 Tbsp parmesan cheese + Italian spices
  • Roll ups: 2 turkey slices + 2 carrot sticks + 1 tsp honey mustard
  • Cucumber sandwiches: 6 cucumber slices + 3 small slices deli meat + 3 small slices cheddar cheese
  • Cucumber crackers: 1 mini or small cheese wedge + 4 cucumber slices + 2 tsp dried cranberries
  • Bell pepper nachos: ½ cup bell pepper slices + 2 Tbsp cheddar cheese + 2 Tbsp sliced black olives (microwave to melt cheese)
  • Zucchini Pizza Bites: 5 zucchini slices + 5 tsp tomato sauce + 3 Tbsp mozzarella cheese (bake at 350 degrees until cheese is melted)
